Brown Rice

Description

Simple and easy brown rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 Tbsp butter cubed

Steps

  1. In a mesh strainer, rinse the brown rice until the water runs clear.
  2. In an appropriately sized pot, combine the rinsed brown rice, salt, and water.
  3. Bring the contents to a boil.
  4. Reduce the stove to low heat. Cover and allow to cook for 1 hour.
  5. After one hour has passed, remove the rice from the heat, and uncover.
  6. Gently fluff rice with a utensil. Add the cubed butter, cover, and allow to sit for 10 minutes.
  7. After 10 minutes has passed, give the rice a good stir.
  8. Serve, keep warm, add to other recipes, or chill.
