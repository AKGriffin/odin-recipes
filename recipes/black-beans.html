Black Beans

Description

Simple adn easy black beans.

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans preferrebly no salt added
  • 1 can tomatoes crushed
  • 1 cup vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 Tbsp butter

Steps

  1. In an appropriately sized pot, melt the butter.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients to the sauce pan.
  3. Reduce heat to low.
  4. Allow pot to cook on low for 2 hours and reduce, stirring occasionally.
  5. Remove from heat. All done
  6. Serve, keep warm, use in other recipes, or chiil
