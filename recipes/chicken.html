Chicken

Description

Simple and easy chicken thighs.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp cider sauce
  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 6 to 8 chicken things no skin, no bones

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a large freezer plastic bag and marinate for at least an hour.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees farenheit.
  3. After marinating, oil a baking sheet and lay chicken thighs out on the sheet.
  4. Bake chicken for 20 minutes.
  5. Remove from the after 20 minutes. Chicken is done once it has reached an internal temperature of 160 degrees farenheit. Cook for an additional 5 minutes and temp. Repeate until ready.
  6. Serve, keep warm, use in other recipes, or chill for later
